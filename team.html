Lyron Co Ting Keh

CEO Seismic

Lyron Co Ting Keh is the founder of Seismic, a company building next-generation encrypted blockchain infrastructure.

He graduated from Stanford University, where he studied computer science and conducted research at the Stanford AI Lab (SAIL). Lyron previously worked at leading technology organizations, including Google X, Apple R&D, and Facebook AI.

Under his leadership, Seismic has raised over $17 million in funding — including two rounds led by a16z Crypto — and is preparing to launch its encrypted blockchain alongside real fintech partners.

Spencer Solit

Founding Engineer

Spencer Solit is a Founding Engineer at Seismic Systems, based in New York, United States. He joined Seismic in 2024, where he focuses on building encrypted blockchain infrastructure and advancing the company’s core cryptographic systems.

Before joining Seismic, Spencer worked as a Research Assistant at the University of Pennsylvania, where he developed zero-knowledge proof protocols for verifying SQL query correctness and integrated advanced cryptography into database architectures. His work included designing PIOP-based proving systems in Rust and creating an SQL planner optimized for proving performance.

He earned both his BASc in Computer Science and Mathematics and his MSE in Computer & Information Science from the University of Pennsylvania, graduating summa cum laude. His core skills include cryptography, Rust and Go development, zero-knowledge proofs, machine learning, and blockchain analysis—positioning him as a key technical leader within Seismic’s engineering team.
